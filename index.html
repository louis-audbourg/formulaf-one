Formula 1

Formula 1, commonly abbreviated to F1, is a motor sport discipline considered to be the premier category of the sport. Over the years it has taken on a global dimension and is, along with the Olympic Games and the Football World Cup, one of the most high-profile sporting events. Every year since 1950, a World Drivers' Championship has been held, supplemented since 1958 by a World Car Manufacturers' Championship. The competition is based on Grand Prix, races in single-seater cars held on closed road circuits that are permanently closed, but sometimes held in cities and temporary, as in Monaco, Singapore, and Baku.
